    About

This mentorship program will be between one 1st+ year and one 1st year woman engineer UTD student for last one academic year. Our mission is to connect women within engineering at UTD and help students feel connected to the university, their major, and other women engineers. We will be planning workshops and events that will develop students professionally, socially, and mentally: we want happy, healthy, successful engineering students who know their dream job and how to go get it!

    Mentors

Apply here to become a mentor! Studying to become an engineer is tough enough without the added struggle of being a woman in a traditionally male-dominated industry. As a UTD student, you learn a lot quickly, and our mentors are shockful of valuable learned lessons. They are eager to share their experiences with incoming freshmen, helping them persevere through the hardships and find a way to achieve their goals. We hope that mentors will be able to develop solid friendships with their mentees, gain confidence in themselves, and grow as leaders.
